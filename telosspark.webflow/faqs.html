Frequently Asked Questions
What is Telos Spark?
An 8 week long web3 accelerator designed to empower and uplift the Telos community.
Who is responsible for running the program?
Telos Spark is an ecosystem initiative supported by the Telos foundation, Symmetric Finance and several other projects from the Telos ecosystem.
Who is eligible for the program?
Anyone interested in launching a decentralized application, infrastructure project or services benefiting the Telos ecosystem is eligible too apply. Each batch will have a specific focus, so please check out our blog for the latest information.
Where can projects submit their application?
Projects are required to submit their application and all supporting materials using the following application form on the F6S platform:
‍
https://www.f6s.com/telos-spark/apply
